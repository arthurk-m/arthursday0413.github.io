The Sad Emoji

Iconography

How do we design with emotion? I was assigned to create icons/emojis that extended the sad emoji beyond its frown.

Through research and interviews I recorded how people reacted to the sad emoji and asked them to reciprocate it in their own ways. Most reacted in a physical expression when the ends of their lips curving downwards. Through this I questioned "Does a physical reaction necessairily prompt an emotional response?" Through this I began to collect images and find stories that illicited a sad emotion. Which led me to understand that context and relatability are crucial to the expression of sadness.

I made vinyl stickers that installed around Providence and class rooms to create conversations around depression and anxiety.
